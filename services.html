Our Services

We provide advanced diagnostics, antitoxin administration, immunotherapy management, and long-term condition support. Our facility is ready to give you quick evaluations and personalized treatment plans, whether you have allergies that keep coming back and could kill you or need specialized care for poisoning.

We also provide other services that extend beyond medical related issues. For someone who needs to stay for an extended period of time we can accomodate you with a stay in our facility. If you're missing a pet that doesn't come home when you call it's name, we can help bring them home through other methods. If someone needs help trying to find out who caused something, they can come to us and we can figure out who the person was that caused the incident.
